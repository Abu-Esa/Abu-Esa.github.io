        <p><strong>The Message</strong> (1976) is a historical epic film directed by Moustapha Akkad. It tells the story of Islam and the life of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), portrayed through the experiences of his followers and the events that shaped the early Muslim community. The film emphasizes the moral and spiritual foundations of Islam while respecting Islamic traditions by not visually depicting the Prophet himself.</p>
